# Neovim Shortcuts
--- 

| Key | Function |
| --- | --- |
| ctrl+y | auto complete |
| *navigation*   |
|down 1 page | ctrl+f |
|up 1 page | ctrl+b |
|up 1/2 page| ctrl+u |
|down 1/2 page| ctrl+d |


## Editing

Yank content from first pair of parenthesis, braces or brackets: 

```yib```

Paste inside pair of parenthesis, braces or brackets: 

```vibp``` *Visually, Inside-of Braces, Paste*
